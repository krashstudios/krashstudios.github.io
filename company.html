Our Story

We believe that our name should reflect our companies work!

After 11 years of working in the human resources industry at a fortune 100 company, I decided that office work was not for me – it was time for a change. I had always enjoyed being outside and working with landscape maintenance and landscaping needs around my own home. This passion inspired my wife and me to start a part-time, two-person landscaping company with “me” as the worker and my wife as the accountant. Four years, and many long, long hours of landscaping later, we decided to make a life-changing jump and leave the fortune 100 company and lifestyle we had been accustomed to, as well as the steady, consistent income and insurance benefits, and start Johnston Landscape Maintenance Inc. This was a large change and a big risk, as our family had grown to include five children. We decided to use our personal name for the company because we believe that our name should reflect our company’s work! I was always taught the importance of keeping your name known for good and I absolutely believe honesty, integrity, and good work ethic will be better remembered in a personal name. This is our family’s reputation and I want ours to be known for honesty and great work! I am proud to put the Johnston name on all of our equipment and work!
