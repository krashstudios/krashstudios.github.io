Landscaping

Whether you own a home or an office building Johnston Landscape Maintenance Inc. can take care of all of your landscaping needs, Johnston Landscapes dependable and professional team will be there for all your projects needs.

Landscaping Services

No matter your landscaping needs Johnston Landscape is equipped to handle your project. Below is a list of some of our services... Don't see what you're looking for, then give us a call for a free consultation.

Lawn Installation and Renovation Planting of Flowers
The most essential part of a landscape is its lawn. Whether you need a new lawn or just some minor renovations to an existing lawn, Johnston Landscape will find an option to fit your budget. We offer sod installation/renovation for an instant green healthy lawn. Another less expensive option is to start a lawn from seed or hydro seeding. No matter which lawn install/renovation you decide to go with Johnston Landscape will handle it from start to finish with the same end result, a thick green healthy lawn! Johnston Landscape offers planting arrangements of annuals or perennials to make your ornamental garden beds stand out with color and beauty.
Shrubs, Hedges and Trees Ground Covering for Garden Beds
Caring for your hedges and shrubs is a vital part of a healthy landscaping. Trimming and pruning not only adds a cosmetic aspect but it is essential for the overall well being of your shrubs and hedges. The team at Johnston Landscape is professionally trained to make sure your hedges and shrubs are pruned and trimmed properly. Increase the curb appeal of your property by adding a decorative ground covering. Johnston Landscape delivers and installs a wide variety of bark mulches and decorative rocks. This not only helps cosmetically but it helps prevent weeds and keeps moisture in the ground.
