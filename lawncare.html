Lawn Care

Johnston Landscape Maintenance Inc. provides lawn care maintenance for commercial and residential properties. We understand how important it is for your property to look its best at all times. Whether it’s the emerging days of spring or the cool autumn days of fall, Johnston Landscape has the right tools and maintenance team to keep your property looking beautiful no matter what season.

Lawn Care Services

We offer customized maintenance programs to fit any budget. Below is a list of some of our services that can be combined to fit any properties particular needs.

Fall Clean Up Spring Clean Up
During a fall clean up we collect all leaves, branches, and debris from the turf area, ornamental and mulch beds. Included in the cleanup is a final lawn mowing (cutting the grass to a low height), which helps promote sturdier root systems throughout the winter. Spring is a great time to spruce up the yard for the new season. At Johnston Landscape, we collect all leaves and miscellaneous debris, and remove them from the property.
Lawn De-Thatching Core Aeration
Remove matted and dead grass to allow the grass to breathe and promote healthy growth. Improves rooting and helps to enhance infiltration of rainfall or irrigation on the lawn.
Weed Control Lawn Mowing
Weekly, bi-weekly or on call weeding services. Johnston Landscape has the knowledge to differentiate between weeds and flowers. Our experienced team can turn any weed infested mulch bed into a well-manicured weed free mulch bed. Weed control is done manually by hand or through the use of herbicides, which are not harmful to the plant materials located in the shrub or mulch beds. Weekly, bi weekly, and on call mowing services. At each lawn-mowing visit, we adjust the mowing height based on turf species and local conditions during the growing season. This helps to ensure a healthy greener looking lawn. All debris are removed from the turf areas prior to mowing and grass clipping are mulched back into your lawn which can supply up to 25 percent of your lawn’s total fertilizer needs. With each mowing, we provide string trimming (lawns are trimmed along borders, flower beds/planters, fence lines, sidewalks and any other areas to ensure a neat appearance.) All clippings are removed from the planting beds, walkways, patios and driveways for a clean professional look and feel.
